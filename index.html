Myntra’s groundbreaking initiative encourages customers to recycle old clothing and take sustainability to new heights. By collecting discarded garments, Myntra re purposes diverse fibers—such as cotton, polyester, and blends—to create fresh, stylish clothing. They even reuse plastics (like PET bottles) to make polyester fibers, diverting plastic waste from landfills. This eco-friendly approach not only promotes conscious fashion but also contributes to a circular economy
